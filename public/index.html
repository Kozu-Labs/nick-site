How referrals work

A clear first step.

Send the non-confidential outline first. The matter can then move through conflicts review, a bounded assessment, and a decision about deeper investigation.

  1. 01

    Outline the opportunity

    Share the public patent or registration number, product category, and what assistance you are considering. Hold confidential facts and accused-party names until conflicts are cleared.

  2. 02

    Define the question

    Clarify whether the immediate need is legal collaboration, a market-surface assessment, or organized factual investigation support.

  3. 03

    Choose the next step

    After conflicts review, determine whether the matter warrants a separate engagement and a more detailed investigation.
