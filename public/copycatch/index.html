03 / RELATIONSHIP

Why it exists

Built from the investigation burden.

Nick co-founded CopyCatch after seeing how much work it takes to turn a broad marketplace problem into an organized factual record that counsel can assess.

CopyCatch can support recurring monitoring, bounded market checks, and deeper factual investigation. It is designed to deliver the work product clients and counsel need to understand the live surface, not to make legal decisions for them.

The boundary

Separate, but connected.

The relationship should be clear before any monitoring, investigation, or legal conversation begins.

01

CopyCatch and Nicholas Lee's law practice are separate but connected. Nick's role at CopyCatch is co-founder and IP litigator. CopyCatch provides monitoring and organized factual investigation support; it does not provide legal advice and does not reach legal conclusions. Its records are produced and reviewed by CopyCatch. Legal services from Nicholas Lee require a conflicts review and a separate engagement.
