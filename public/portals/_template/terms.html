What this site is

This portal is a service-of-process record provided pursuant to the Court's order authorizing alternative service in case __CASE_NUMBER__ in the United States District Court for the Northern District of Illinois.

It allows defendants identified in the operative complaint to view and download court filings in their case. Access is restricted to the email address(es) the plaintiff has on file for each defendant, in combination with the case-specific password provided by counsel.

Access logging

For evidentiary purposes, the following information is recorded each time you sign in or interact with the portal:

  • The email address used to sign in (whether or not the sign-in succeeded);
  • The IP address of the device making the request;
  • The browser/User-Agent string of the device;
  • The server-side timestamp of each event;
  • Which documents you view or download.

The access log is retained as part of the firm's record of effective service of process and may be submitted to the Court in support of any motion for default judgment.
