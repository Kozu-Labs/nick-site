# Attorney Advertising Pre-Publication Checklist

Use this checklist for every new or materially changed public page, article, bio, result reference, campaign, and CopyCatch surface that names or depicts Nicholas Lee. Record the page URL, reviewer, date, and approval in the release note.

## Identity And Credentials

- [ ] Name, title, firm, admissions, education, location, and years in practice match a verified source.
- [ ] No `specialist`, `expert`, certification, or superiority language appears without documented authorization.
- [ ] Nick is presented as a practicing IP litigator and CopyCatch co-founder, not as the reviewer or supervisor of a specific CopyCatch output.

## Claims And Results

- [ ] No guarantee, win rate, recovery estimate, performance number, or comparative claim appears without a current approved substantiation record.
- [ ] No `court-ready`, `litigation-grade`, admissibility, or chain-of-custody claim appears unless the underlying product contract and legal approval both exist.
- [ ] Past matters or results are public-record corroborated, jurisdictionally compliant, contextualized, and approved by Nick before publication.
- [ ] Every quote, accolade, award, logo, and testimonial is authentic and permitted for this use.

## CopyCatch Boundary

- [ ] CopyCatch is identified as a separate company providing monitoring and organized factual investigation, not legal advice or legal conclusions.
- [ ] Nick appears as origin and provenance, never as implied supervision of an output unless a recorded review event supports the exact statement.
- [ ] Only event-backed review labels render. Until additional contracts exist, the only approved output label is `CopyCatch reviewed`.
- [ ] Legal services are described as requiring conflicts review and a separate engagement.

## Intake And Privacy

- [ ] Legal intake asks only for a non-confidential outline and public identifiers before conflicts review.
- [ ] The intake surface tells visitors not to send accused-party names, confidential facts, legal strategy, or evidence.
- [ ] Monitoring-only visitors visibly leave the law-practice path; any later legal handoff requires fresh consent and conflicts review.
- [ ] Form delivery, rate limiting, honeypot behavior, Slack routing, and storage are tested without exposing secrets or client data.

## Search And Distribution

- [ ] Title, description, canonical URL, Open Graph data, sitemap, and `llms.txt` accurately describe the page without expanding its claims.
- [ ] Private portals and client material remain `noindex, nofollow, noarchive` at both crawler and hosting-header layers.
- [ ] Structured data contains verified facts only and does not imply ratings, results, clients, or credentials that are not on the page.
